Priorities

S.M.A.R.T. Leadership for West Orange

Kenneth isn't running to be a politician. He's running to listen, to demand accountability for how tax dollars are spent, and to bring disciplined, common-sense leadership to Town Hall.

What residents are telling us

Rising property taxes and cost-of-living affordability
Small businesses struggling under taxes and red tape
Government transparency deficits
Senior and special-needs family support gaps
Roads, traffic, parking, and safety quality-of-life matters
Development oversight to protect neighborhoods
S

Savings — Lower taxes and affordability

  • Eliminate wasteful spending
  • Strengthen financial oversight
  • Avoid debt-increasing decisions
  • Review major developments and PILOT programs for taxpayer impact
M

Management — Business-minded leadership

  • Streamline licensing and regulations
  • Establish a one-stop Business Concierge Service at Town Hall
  • Run the town efficiently, like a well-managed organization
A

Accountability — Responsible oversight

  • Transparent financial reporting
  • Careful review of major development decisions
  • Support only responsible growth that strengthens the tax base
R

Roads & Residents — Safer, stronger communities

  • Address road conditions, traffic, parking, and rodent issues
  • Support seniors and special-needs families
  • Practical, everyday quality-of-life fixes
T

Transparency — Open and honest government

  • Accessible, plain-English financial reporting
  • Clear visibility into how and why decisions are made
  • Rebuild resident trust in local government
