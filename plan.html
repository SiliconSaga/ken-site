A Resident-First Proposal

The Valley P.A.R.K. Plan

Practical solutions for parking, public safety, accountability, and neighborhood quality of life in West Orange.

Share Your Concern Get Involved

The Valley is one of the most important neighborhoods in West Orange. It is home to families, seniors, workers, homeowners, renters, small businesses, and institutions — and it deserves a plan that takes its daily realities seriously.

This is about parking. But it is also about safety. It is about accountability. It is about zoning. It is about neighborhood respect. It is about making sure West Orange remains livable for the people who actually live here.

What P.A.R.K. Stands For

Four pillars. One resident-first plan for the Valley — and every neighborhood in West Orange.

P

Parking

Practical, fair solutions to the parking pressure that makes daily life harder for residents, visitors, and small businesses in the Valley.

A

Accountability

Decisions made in the open, with real answers for residents — not promises that quietly disappear after election day.

R

Rules

Consistent zoning enforcement and common-sense ordinances that protect neighborhoods instead of burdening the people who follow them.

K

Keeping neighborhoods livable

Public safety, quality of life, and respect for the character of the Valley and every West Orange neighborhood.
