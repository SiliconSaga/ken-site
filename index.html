Why This Race Matters

This Race Is About Trust, Taxes, and Accountability

West Orange is a community we are proud to call home. But residents are raising serious concerns about rising property taxes, affordability, business pressure, transparency in government, overdevelopment, and everyday quality-of-life issues that too often go unresolved.

Kenneth Gibbons is running because residents deserve leadership that understands the numbers, asks harder questions, and puts the community before insiders and politics as usual.

What residents are telling us

  • Rising property taxes & affordability
  • Small businesses being squeezed out
  • Lack of trust and transparency at Town Hall
  • Overdevelopment & PILOT agreements
  • Roads, traffic, parking, and rodents
  • Support for seniors and special-needs families

The Case for Kenneth
