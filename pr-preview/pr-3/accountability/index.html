The Accountability Agenda

Let's Shine a Light on West Orange's Finances

A serious oversight plan from a Certified Public Accountant, auditor, and lifelong resident. Lower taxes. Clearer reporting. Honest decisions. Resident-first leadership.

Support the Campaign Join the Team

Local government runs on trust — and trust is built one disciplined decision at a time. The Accountability Agenda is the standard Kenneth Gibbons will bring to West Orange Township Council: ask the hard questions, demand the numbers, protect taxpayers, and make sure residents always know what's being done in their name.

Lower Taxes & Responsible Budgeting

Residents face some of the highest effective property tax rates in New Jersey, and real relief starts with disciplined budgeting.

  • Line-by-line municipal budget review to eliminate waste
  • Professional auditing standards for major expenditures
  • Advocacy for multi-year fiscal planning
  • Opposition to hidden long-term debt creation

Clearer Financial Reporting

Residents shouldn't need a CPA to understand where their tax dollars go.

  • Resident-friendly budget summaries
  • Published major contracts and PILOT terms
  • Straightforward spending dashboards
  • Harder council questions on variances and overruns

Reviewing Major Development Decisions

Proposals like Prism, Rock Springs, and Stonehill carry lasting consequences.

  • Independent fiscal-impact analysis requirement
  • School capacity, traffic, and safety evaluation
  • Support for responsible growth that strengthens the tax base
  • Opposition to overdevelopment

Responsible Use of PILOT Agreements

PILOT (Payment In Lieu Of Taxes) agreements can be useful tools — only when structured responsibly.

  • Transparent PILOT disclosure
  • School impact evaluation
  • Fair revenue-sharing terms
  • Rejection of PILOTs that don't benefit residents

Stronger Oversight & Accountability

Kenneth brings 30+ years of audit and financial-review experience to council deliberations.

  • Financial statement review in council deliberations
  • Independent reviews when red flags appear
  • Strengthened internal controls
  • Standard accountability questioning

Better Support for Small Businesses

West Orange's small businesses are getting squeezed — by high taxes, costly licensing, and unnecessary red tape.

  • A Business Concierge Service at Town Hall
  • Streamlined licensing and permitting
  • Mercantile license ordinance review
  • A partnership approach to business support

Quality-of-Life Improvements

As a caregiver and the parent of a child with special needs, Kenneth understands what residents are actually living with.

  • Practical fixes for transportation issues
  • Serious rodent and sanitation responses
  • Enhanced senior and special-needs support
  • Public-safety attention for the Valley neighborhood

Transparent Resident Communication

Trust starts with communication.

  • Clearer agenda summaries
  • Earlier publication of meeting materials
  • Pre-decision explanations
  • Genuine integration of resident questions
  • Showing up, listening, and responding consistently

Elect an Auditor — Not an Insider

West Orange deserves disciplined, transparent, resident-first leadership.
