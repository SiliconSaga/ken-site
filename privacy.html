Legal

Privacy Policy

Gibbons for West Orange ("the campaign," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us — for example, when you email the campaign, volunteer, respond to the resident survey, sign up for the community bulletin, or make a contribution. That may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and the comments you share.

How we use it

  • To respond to your questions and follow up on volunteer or event interest
  • To send campaign updates you've asked to receive
  • To comply with New Jersey campaign-finance reporting requirements for contributions

Donations

Online contributions are processed by Anedot, a secure third-party payment processor. The campaign does not store your full payment-card details. Contribution information is handled in accordance with applicable campaign-finance law.

What we don't do

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as required to operate the campaign (for example, with the payment processor) or as required by law.
