Kenneth Gibbons Announces Campaign for West Orange Township Council

January 15, 2025

CPA Kenneth Gibbons — the third generation of his family to own a home in West Orange — has officially launched his campaign for West Orange Township Council, pledging to fight for lower taxes, accountability, and community values.
